Daniels Final

Welcome to Daniels final project where I will use this opportunity to make a page about one of my interest the classic car. I have loved classic cars since I was young when my parents took me to the Gilmore Car Museum for the first time. I have been back several times since that first time. I one-day hope to restore my own classic car like a 1969 Ford Mustang or a 1965 Pontiac GTO for example. Throughout this website I will have you view several photos of classic cars, get some basic information, fill out a form about classic cars and offer some contact information that can be used if you have any interest in learning more about classic cars.
